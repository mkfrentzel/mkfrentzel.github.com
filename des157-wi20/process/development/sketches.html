Interaction Goals

In general my main goal is to educate my audience about the history of fake news in an interactive and engaging way. I hope to use a lot of scrolling features to make elements fade in and out of the page, as well as clickable elements to help populate information on the page. This way the layout will still look clean and minimalistic, but will be able to inform the viewer. It will also allow them to engage with the sections they are most interested in and get them the information efficiently.
